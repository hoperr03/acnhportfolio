hello and welcome to my little portfolio of my childhood game, animal crossing! These snapshots are of my animal crossing new horizons island specifically. i really like the aesthetics of the games and the simplicity of them. you don't have to "beat" the game, don't have to do things at a fast pace, you can just take it easy and do things to your own discretion. new horizons really took off during the pandemic in 2020 and was widely popular when everyone was stuck in the house. i started playing animal crossing wild world at around nine years old and have been playing the games ever since.
